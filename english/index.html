About us

The company "Bella flor" is located in the city of Zaragoza (Spain). Bella flor has extensive experience with the support of several years within the business, as well as different activities such as; flower distribution, plant distribution, flower product distribution including foundations, paints, stains, glues, flower arrangement products, water absorbent foams for plant conservation, floral product design for events, image design and arrangements. On the other hand, it offers attention to companies and individuals in terms of floral design and recruitment in floristry issues. It has several physical stores throughout Zaragoza.
